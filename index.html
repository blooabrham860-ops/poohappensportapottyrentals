Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does it cost to rent a porta potty for a day?

The cost to rent a porta potty for a day in the United States typically includes a base delivery and pickup fee, making it similar to a weekend rate. While prices vary by region, we offer flat-rate packages for short-term needs to keep the rental cost of porta potty nationwide affordable for single-day events.

2. How many portable toilets do I need for my event?

For a standard 4-hour event, we recommend one porta potty rental in the United States for every 50 guests. If you are serving alcohol or the event lasts longer, you should increase the number of portable toilet units by about 20% to prevent long lines and ensure the tanks don't fill up too quickly.

3. Do portable toilets require electricity or water?

Standard port o potty rental units in the United States are self-contained and do not require external connections. However, luxury portable restroom rental trailers typically need a standard 110v electrical outlet and a garden hose connection for water to power the lights, AC, and flushing mechanisms.

4. How often are the units serviced?

For long-term portable toilet rental for construction sites in the United States, we typically service the units once a week. This involves pumping out the waste, cleaning the interior, and recharging the deodorizing fluid. For high-traffic events, we can arrange for daily portable toilet servicing to maintain freshness.

5. Can I rent a unit in winter?

Yes, we offer portable toilet rental nationwide year-round. In colder climates, we add a specialized anti-freeze brine to the waste tank to prevent freezing. We also offer heaters for portable bathroom rental trailers to ensure user comfort during winter months.
